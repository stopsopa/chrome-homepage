# Privacy Policy for Custom New Tab Redirect

**Effective Date: Today**

## Introduction

Custom New Tab Redirect is a Chrome extension designed to let you customize your new tab page by redirecting it to any URL. We value your privacy and have built this extension to be completely secure and private. This policy explains how our extension handles your data.

## Data Collection and Usage

### What Data We Do NOT Collect

Custom New Tab Redirect does **NOT** collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to external servers.

- We do **not** track your browsing history.
- We do **not** collect your search queries.
- We do **not** collect any personal identification information.
- We do **not** use any third-party analytics services.

### Local Data Usage

The extension operates entirely locally on your device. It accesses the following information solely to provide its core functionality:

1.  **Custom URL**: The URL you provide in the extension's popup to serve as your new tab redirect.

### Local Data Storage

We use your browser's standard `localStorage` specific to the extension to save your custom URL. 

This data never leaves your browser, is sandboxed within the extension, and is not shared with anyone or any third party.

## Data Security

Since Custom New Tab Redirect does not transmit data to any external servers and requires zero special permissions, your data remains 100% secure on your local device.

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through the Chrome Web Store listing.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository issues page.
