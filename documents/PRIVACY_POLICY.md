# Privacy Policy for Custom New Tab Dashboard

**Effective Date: Today**

## Introduction

Custom New Tab Dashboard is a Chrome extension designed to help you organize your productivity through a custom new tab page. We value your privacy and have built this extension to be secure and private. This policy explains how our extension handles your data.

## Data Collection and Usage

### What Data We Do NOT Collect

Custom New Tab Dashboard does **NOT** collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to external servers.

- We do **not** track your browsing history.
- We do **not** collect or transmit your search queries to any third party (queries are only sent directly to the search engines you explicitly select).
- We do **not** collect any personal identification information.
- We do **not** use any third-party analytics services or trackers.

### Local Data Usage

The extension operates entirely locally on your device. It uses the following local resources solely to provide its core functionality:

1.  **Dashboard Settings**: Your search engine selections and search queries are stored in your browser's local storage to persist between sessions.
2.  **Bookmarks**: Your dashboard links and "AI Skills" are stored in a dedicated bookmark folder (named `_`) on your local device.
3.  **Active Search**: When you perform a search, the extension opens the selected search engines/AI tools in new tabs.

### Local Data Storage

- We use your browser's standard `localStorage` and `bookmarks` APIs to save your configurations.
- This data never leaves your browser, is sandboxed within the extension environment, and is not shared with any external entity.

## Data Security

Since Custom New Tab Dashboard does not transmit data to any external servers, your configurations and prompts remain secure on your local device. The extension uses standard browser security models to ensure that its data is isolated from other websites.

## Third-Party Services

This extension allows you to interact with third-party search engines and AI platforms (such as Google, ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.). When you perform a search, you are redirected to these services directly. Your interactions with those services are governed by their respective privacy policies.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through the Chrome Web Store listing.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository issues page.
